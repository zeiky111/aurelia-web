Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum age for enrollment?

We offer programs for ages 4 and up. Kids English starts from age 4, while General English programs are available from age 8 onwards. We also have adult programs.

How often are classes held?

Classes can be scheduled from 1-5 times per week depending on your preference. Most students do 2-3 sessions weekly for optimal progress.

What if I miss a class?

We offer makeup sessions. If you miss a class, you can reschedule for another time that week or within the enrollment period, subject to availability.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! Students who complete full programs receive Aurelia English Center certificates. Advanced students can also take international exams for globally recognized certifications.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

If you're not satisfied within the first 2 weeks, we offer a full refund. We're confident you'll love learning with us!

Can I transfer to a different class or instructor?

Yes, if you're not compatible with your current class or instructor, we can arrange a transfer to another session or instructor at no extra cost.
