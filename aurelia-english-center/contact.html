Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll?

Visit our center, call us, or fill out the contact form. We'll schedule a free assessment and help you choose the perfect program.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free trial class so you can experience our teaching style and meet our instructors before enrolling.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and online payments. Flexible payment plans are available for longer programs.

Can I change my schedule?

Yes, schedule changes are allowed with proper notice. Contact us to modify your class times.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, students receive course completion certificates. International exam preparation programs include official certification.

What if I miss a class?

Students can reschedule missed classes or access recorded sessions. Make-up classes are available within the month.
