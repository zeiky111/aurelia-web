Our Story

Aurelia English Center was founded with a singular mission: to transform how students learn and use English. Based in the vibrant city of Koronadal, Mindanao, Philippines 🇵🇭, we've been serving students and families since 2020.

Our name, Aurelia, represents the golden light of knowledge and opportunity. Just as our logo symbolizes guidance and brilliance, we guide every student toward their English excellence and international success.

We believe that language learning is not just about grammar and vocabulary—it's about building confidence, enabling communication, and opening doors to global opportunities.
